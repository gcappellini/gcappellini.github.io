Biography

I am Guglielmo Cappellini, a researcher and musician based in Italy. My academic journey began with an MSc in Nuclear Engineering, followed by a PhD in Artificial Intelligence for Health. I am currently a postdoctoral researcher, focusing on the application of AI to healthcare.

Professional Interests

  • Artificial Intelligence for healthcare and medicine
  • Data science and machine learning
  • Interdisciplinary research and innovation

Music

Alongside my research, I am an independent drummer, performing and recording with various bands and artists. My experience spans studio recording, live concerts, videoclips, and film soundtracks.
